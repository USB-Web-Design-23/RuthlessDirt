The Olympic National Park

The Olympic National Park, on the Olympic Peninsula west of Seattle, feautures many beautiful landscapes. Covering nearly a million acres, visit places like the Hoh Rainforest, or beautiful Lake Crescent. You can hike, bike, camp, stay at Lake Crescent, swim, and much much more. Personally, I find the Hoh Rainforest absolutely breathtaking and the views on Lake Crescent spectacular.
